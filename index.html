Your experience with us

Nestled amidst the enchanting landscapes of Hiriwaduna, our boutique hotel in Sri Lanka offers an unparalleled retreat where nature's beauty and modern comfort harmoniously converge. Set against the backdrop of a serene lake and enveloped by lush forest, our hotel provides a tranquil escape for those seeking to immerse themselves in the splendor of the island's wilderness. Wake up to the gentle rustling of leaves and the melodious symphony of native birds, as the rising sun casts its golden glow upon the tranquil waters. Each elegantly designed room is a sanctuary of relaxation, offering breathtaking views of the verdant surroundings. Whether indulging in a leisurely stroll along the forest trails, embarking on a serene boat ride across the glistening lake, or simply unwinding on your private veranda, every moment is a celebration of nature's embrace. Experience the magic of Hiriwaduna from the comfort of our lakeside hotel, where the serenity of the forest and the allure of the waters combine to create an unforgettable escape.

History

Nestled within the tranquil embrace of Hiriwaduna, Sri Lanka, lies the Whispering Woods Lodge, a testament to the rich history and natural beauty of the region. With roots dating back to the ancient civilizations that once flourished here, the hotel stands as a gateway to an enchanting past and a serene present. Situated amidst lush greenery and vibrant flora, the hotel offers guests an immersive experience that seamlessly blends modern comforts with echoes of a bygone era. Hiriwaduna, with its storied past as a thriving agricultural village, provides the perfect backdrop for the hotel's architecture and ambiance, paying homage to the indigenous culture and traditions that have shaped the area. With personalized service, luxurious accommodations, and a commitment to preserving the environment, Hiriwaduna Oasis Hotel invites travelers to embark on a journey of discovery, where the whispers of history and the whispers of nature harmonize in captivating symphony.
